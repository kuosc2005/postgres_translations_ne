# NDFP Nepali PostgreSQL Glossary v0.1

Working glossary for AI-assisted Nepali PostgreSQL message translation. Native Nepali review and PostgreSQL technical review are mandatory.

## Policy

- SQL keywords, utility names, command names, options, placeholders, protocol names, and format names stay in English.
- Deep PostgreSQL technical terms usually stay in English or are transliterated until reviewed.
- Ordinary operational words are translated into simple Nepali.
- `[REVIEW: ...]` markers are temporary and must not remain in final `.po` files.
